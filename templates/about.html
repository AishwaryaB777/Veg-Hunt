Welcome to VegHunt, your go-to platform for discovering and reviewing vegetarian restaurants. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly space where food lovers can explore the best vegetarian dining options with authentic reviews and ratings. We are a team of four passionate B.Tech students—Agraja, Aiswarya P., Aiswarya Sony, and Aiswarya B. Nair—working on this project as part of our academic journey. VegHunt is our mini project, designed to bridge the gap between vegetarian food enthusiasts and quality restaurants by offering a well-organized and informative directory. Driven by technology and a shared love for good food, we strive to make VegHunt a trusted platform for discovering the best vegetarian experience
